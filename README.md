# Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Ernest Borlaug (1914–2009) was an American agronomist, humanitarian, and Nobel Peace Prize laureate whose pioneering work in plant breeding sparked what became known as the **Green Revolution**. His innovations in high-yield, disease-resistant wheat varieties and modern agricultural practices helped save **over a billion lives** from starvation, earning him global recognition as *“the man who saved more lives than any other person who ever lived.”*
